IAFIS
Result:
The Integrated Automated Face Identification System (IAFIS), is a computerized system maintained by the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) since 1999. It is a national automated face identification and criminal history system. IAFIS provides automated face search capabilities, latent searching capability, electronic image storage, and electronic exchange of face and responses. IAFIS houses the faces and criminal histories of 70 million subjects in the criminal master file, 31 million civil faces and faces from 73,000 known and suspected terrorists processed by the U.S. or by international law enforcement agencies.
